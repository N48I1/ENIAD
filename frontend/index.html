About ENIAD Cert

The National School of Artificial Intelligence and Digital (ENIAD) leverages cutting-edge blockchain technology to secure academic credentials.

Immutable

Once issued, certificates cannot be altered or deleted without a trace. The blockchain provides a permanent record.

Global Verification

Employers and institutions worldwide can instantly verify the authenticity of a diploma using its unique hash.

Fraud Proof

Cryptographic signatures prevent forgery. Only authorized university admin wallets can issue valid certificates.
